CONTEXT

A group of friends and I all worked on this project collectively. The story was built from a reddit post. The film was entered in multiple contests and festivals. It won best of show in both district and state level at SkillsUSA, nominated as a highschool short in the Dallas International Film Festival, and won "Best Visual Effects" in the Plano West Film Festival.
