CONTEXT

This is a recreation of Edgar Wright's Baby Driver. We originally made it as a fun project to show off to our friends in film club. This was all done in two days. One day for shooting, the other for editing. We ended up winning best film in our club and went on to win "Best Shot for Shot" in the Plano West Film Festival.
